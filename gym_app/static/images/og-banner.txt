Groei App - Your Workout Tracker

Track your workouts, monitor progress, and grow stronger with Groei - a minimalist but powerful workout tracker designed for serious lifters.

Features:
- Track sets, reps and weight for each exercise
- Monitor your progress with beautiful visualizations
- View your workout history in a GitHub-style activity heatmap
- Organize exercises by muscle groups
